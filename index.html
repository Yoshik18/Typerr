Hey there fellas, Typer is a fun speed typing game. In order to score you need to type the word specified in the given amount of seconds!! As your score the the difficulty of the game increases, watch the time :). If you fail to type the word in given amount of time you lose and your score resets to zero. To restart the game just reload the web page. You can also view your high/best score, after the game ends the button will automatically appear on the screen.
